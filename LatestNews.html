ASHS Basketball Team Heads to Tournament

Our school basketball team is gearing up for an exciting tournament this weekend. After weeks of training and dedication, the players are ready to showcase their skills and represent ASHS with pride. The tournament will bring together schools from across the region, offering our athletes a chance to compete at a high level and gain valuable experience. Win or lose, this is a fantastic opportunity for our team to grow, bond, and inspire others in the ASHS community.
