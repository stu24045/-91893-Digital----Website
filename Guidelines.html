ASHS Gym Guidelines

Safety Rules
  • Always warm up before starting your workout.
  • Use all equipment as instructed.
  • Report any injuries or hazards to nurse/teacher immediately.
  • Stay hydrated and take breaks as needed.
Gym Etiquette
  • Wipe down machines after use.
  • Share equipment during busy times.
  • Keep noise to a respectful level.
  • Respect other members space and time.
Dress Code
  • Wear appropriate gym attire and footwear.
  • No open-toed shoes or sandals allowed.
  • Use towel when needed.
Membership Rules
  • Gym is open Monday to Friday, 7:00am - 8:30am and 12:40pm - 1:30pm.
  • Guests must be registered with sports department.
  • 50$ per year membership fee
